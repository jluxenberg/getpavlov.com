Scary sounds

Fireworks, doors opening and closing, metal clanging, people talking; noises that fade into the background for humans can have a totally different impact on our furry friends.

No more fear

Pavlov helps your dog learn to tolerate sounds they find scary or threatening. It's like a workout for your dog!

A game for your dog to learn

Simply record a sample of the sounds that trigger your dog's anxiety using your phone, then start a training session in the app. The app will play a sound and dispense a treat, teaching your dog to associate the trigger sound with a tasty treat!

During the workout, the delay between the trigger sound and the treat is slowly reduced. This gives your dog a mental workout and keeps her from becoming scared during the training process. This is key to a successful training session, because she can only learn when she feels safe.
